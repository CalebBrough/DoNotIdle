Do Not Idle! - The Game

About the Game:

Do Not Idle is a game about raising awareness about idling to help change the climate so we can keep our planet clean.

Instructions/Movements:

  • Use the left and right arrow keys to drive the car.
  • Use the up arrow key to turn on the car, and the down arrow key to turn it off.

At each level, you need to complete a task, but you need to make sure you don't idle when you stop moving!

Here's the game! Have fun!

About Each Level and Why It's Included:

Level 1: This level is an example of a drive-thru at McDonalds (or any other fast-food restaurant), one of the places that most drivers idle without even thinking about it, and with a world-wide average wait time of about six minutes, there's lots of time to save some gas!

Level 2: This level is another very common place where drivers idle. This level is an example of a drop-off situation, where there isn't a ton of idling, but there is still some gas to save by turning off your car.

Level 3: Within the drop-off-pick-up group, pick-up areas are very prone to idling. When picking up a student from school, or any extra-curricular activity, it's always a smart idea to turn off your car and help save our planet.

Remember... Save the gas--don't pass it!
